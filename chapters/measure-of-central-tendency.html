8.1 Introduction

This chapter explains important statistical concepts related to the analysis and interpretation of data. Statistical tools help summarize, organize, and draw conclusions from collected data.

Understanding these concepts allows researchers, scientists, and analysts to make informed decisions based on numerical evidence.

8.2 Key Concepts

Statistical analysis often involves summarizing large datasets into meaningful indicators such as averages, dispersion, and trends.

8.2.1 Important Measures

  • Mean
  • Median
  • Mode
  • Variance
  • Standard Deviation
Mean = (Σx) / n
Variance = Σ (x − μ)² / n
Standard Deviation = √Variance
